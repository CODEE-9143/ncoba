Minister's Message

Welcome to the Department of Education. As Minister, I am proud to lead a team dedicated to transforming education across Papua New Guinea...

Our vision for 2075 is bold and inclusive, ensuring every child has access to quality learning regardless of location or background...

We are investing in infrastructure, teacher training, and digital tools to empower both students and educators...

Community involvement is key. I encourage parents, guardians, and local leaders to actively participate in shaping our schools...

Transparency and accountability remain central to our mission. We will continue to publish reports and updates regularly...

Thank you for your continued support. Together, we can build a brighter future for our children and our nation.

Director's Message

As Director of Education, I am honored to work alongside passionate educators and administrators across the country...

Our department is focused on delivering curriculum that is relevant, inclusive, and forward-thinking...

We are rolling out new teacher support programs and digital platforms to enhance classroom experiences...

Monitoring and evaluation are essential. We are strengthening our data systems to track progress and improve outcomes...

We believe in lifelong learning. Our initiatives extend beyond schools to vocational and adult education programs...

Let us continue to collaborate, innovate, and inspire. Education is the foundation of national development.
