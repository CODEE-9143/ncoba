The Bougainville Peace Agreement is the foundation of our mandate.

NCOBA ensures its provisions are respected, implemented, and monitored.

We coordinate with both governments to uphold commitments and timelines.

Implementation includes legal reforms, capacity building, and public education.

We also track progress and report outcomes to stakeholders.

This work ensures that peace is not just promised‚Äîbut delivered.

Strengthen national-Bougainville coordination

Coordination between governments is essential for unity and progress.

NCOBA facilitates regular dialogue, joint planning, and shared resources.

We promote mutual understanding and respect for autonomy.

Coordination helps prevent conflict and ensures policy alignment.

It also builds trust and strengthens democratic institutions.

Our role is to be the bridge that connects vision with action.

Support socio-economic development

Socio-economic growth is vital for peace and prosperity.

NCOBA supports initiatives in agriculture, education, and entrepreneurship.

We work with local leaders to identify priorities and mobilize resources.

Development programs are designed to be inclusive and sustainable.

We also monitor impact and adjust strategies as needed.

Our goal is to uplift communities and reduce inequality.

Innovation drives progress and adapts governance to changing needs.
